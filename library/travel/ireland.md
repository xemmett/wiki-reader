# Ireland

Ireland is an island in the North Atlantic, separated from Great Britain to its
east by the North Channel, the Irish Sea, and St George's Channel.

## Landscape

The island's lush green interior — the source of its nickname, the Emerald Isle
— is ringed by rugged coastal cliffs and mountains. The Cliffs of Moher and the
Ring of Kerry are among its best-known natural attractions.

## Culture

Ireland has a rich literary and musical tradition, producing writers such as
James Joyce, W. B. Yeats, and Samuel Beckett. Traditional Irish music remains a
living part of everyday life in pubs and festivals across the country.
